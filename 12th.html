Making Tea

1.Boil water in pot

2.Add a tea leaves and sugar.

3.let it simmer for few minutes.

4.Strain the tea into a cup and add a milk if desired

making Parata and Aloo curry

1.Knead that dough for parata and let it test.

2.Roll out the dough and cook on a hot giddle with oil or ghee.

3.For aloo curry,saute onions,tomatoes,and spices in a pan.

4.Add boiled potatoes and cook until the curry thickens.

Making Biryani

1.Fiest Ready for biryani things and cutting vegetables with pieces.

2.Cook basmathi rice until it's 70% done.

3.Layer the Marinated meat/vegetables and rice in a pot.

4.Cook on the low(dum) until fully cooked.
